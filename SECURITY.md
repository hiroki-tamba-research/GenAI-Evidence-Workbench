# Security and privacy policy

This public repository must not contain real LLM exports, conversation text, attachments, honeypot values, credentials, customer identifiers, proprietary detection rules, or investigation workpapers.

If you discover sensitive material in the repository, do not open a public issue containing the material. Contact the maintainer through the contact route on the [GitHub profile](https://github.com/hiroki-tamba-research) and provide only the minimum location and hash needed to identify it.

Potential third-party data is handled under a safe-stop policy: preserve only the identifier, location, type, and SHA-256 required for auditability, and do not inspect or reproduce the content.
